Courses We Offer

Two Year Higher Secondary Courses in Arts, Science and Commerce.
Three Year Degree Courses (TDC) in Arts, Science and Commerce.

Higher Secondary

In Higher Secondary course we have Arts, Science and Commerce streams.

Subject we offer in Arts stream: English, MIL (Bengali)/Alt. English, Economics, Political Science, History, Mathematics, Logic & Philosophy, Computer Science & Applications, Arabic, Sanskrit, Advance Bengali and Statistics.

Subject we offer in Science stream: English, MIL (Bengali)/Alt. English, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Statistics, Computer Science & Applications.

Subject we offer in Commerce stream: English, MIL (Bengali)/Alt. English, Accountancy, Business Study, Economics, Commercial Mathematics & Statistics (CMS), Mathematics, Computer Science & Applications.

Three Year Degree(TDC)

In Three Years Degree Course we have Arts, Science and Commerce streams with Facilities for Honours are available.

Subjects we offer for Honours

In Science stream: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology, Statistics, Computer Science, BCA and Bio-technology

In Commerce stream: Accountancy & Taxation, Business Management, Banking & Finance.

In Arts stream: English, Bengali, Economics, Political Science, History, Mathematics, Philosophy, Arabic, Sanskrit
